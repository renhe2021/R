# Chapter 2: Technical Analysis Signals

Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD) is a trend-following momentum indicator. When the MACD line crosses above the signal line, it generates a bullish signal. RSI (Relative Strength Index) below 30 indicates oversold conditions.
A common technical screening rule: RSI < 30 AND MACD bullish crossover AND Price > MA200. This combines momentum and trend analysis for better entry timing.
